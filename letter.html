A Letter from the Director

Congratulations, candidate. You have reached the true end of your evaluation. You should be proud.

I sincerely thank you for your interest in the program. I am deeply humbled by your enthusiasm, and impressed by your ingenuity.

Due to the nature of the program, I cannot be there to congratulate you in person, but rest assured, your achievements haven't gone unnoticed.

The success of this program is entirely dependent on candidates such as yourself.
Should you know of anyone who may be interested in participating, we would be pleased to receive their application.


The selection process for this program is still ongoing. While you wait for the results, you may wish to take a look at our upcoming programs and follow their progress.


May the Patron guide your way.
Director
